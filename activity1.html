How to make French Toast

French Toast is toast made by the French so you know it's got an attitude. Just Kidding! French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs, sugar and typically milk, then pan fried. Alternative names and variants include "eggy bread", "Bombay toast", "gypsy toast",and "poor knights" (of Windsor).

Ingredients

(4 Servings)

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 4 Slices of Bread

Step By Step

  1. To make this easy French Toast recipe: Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk

  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly

  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired. Voila, easy French toast

  4. Easy Spiced Syrup: Add 1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract and 1/4 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup; stir well to mix. Serve warm, if desired.
